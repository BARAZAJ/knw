Our Mission Objectives

Protect & Preserve

Safeguarding the natural ecosystem of Lake Kyoga and River Nile through sustainable environmental protection and community engagement.

Raise Awareness

To promote understanding of the importance of River Nile and Lake Kyoga by educating communities about environmental protection.

Build Partnerships

To collaborate with government agencies and organizations to develop alliance strengthening our conservation efforts through collaboration.
